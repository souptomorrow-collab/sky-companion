
To collect every Winged Light, players need to play the level twice as there are two different ways to the bottom.

Note: ''On a Winged Light run, slide down the Lower Valley Race until the Child of Light is collected, teleport Home, then meditate to return to Valley. Then back out of the Lower Valley Race room and return to the Frozen Lake, and head to the Citadel and Upper Valley Race.''

===Valley Rest ({{#var:valley_rest}})<span id="Valley Rest"></span>===
At the exit from the Valley Rest area, a large slope will stretch down and offer several sliding paths.

*'''Valley WL 1''': As soon as the slide starts, aim for the roofed area on the left. The Child of Light is standing in the tunnel. Unlike Children of Light in other realms, this one will be collected automatically by sliding through or near it.

===Frozen Lake ({{#var:frozen_lake}})<span id="First Slope"><span id="Icerink"><span id="Frozen Lake"></span></span></span>===
Taking any path on the large slope from the Valley Rest will lead to the Frozen Lake area.

*'''Valley WL 2''': The Child of Light is standing on the platform in the middle of the Frozen Lake.
*'''Valley WL 3''': Slide into one of the frozen-over holes in the ice to get beneath the rink. The Child of Light is standing on a hill by a sunken boat, near where the light shines in from above.

===Lower Valley Track ({{#var:lower_valley_track}})<span id="Sliding Race"><span id="Lower Valley Track"></span></span>===
Through the Frozen Lake, take the exit with the red banners that doesn't have a bridge connected to it. This exit leads to the Lower Valley Track.

<gallery widths="125" spacing="small" captionalign="center" orientation="landscape" hideaddbutton="true" mode="nolines">

StarValley09.jpg|Valley WL 4 (Auto-collection)
</gallery>

*'''Valley WL 4''': The Child of Light is located near the end of the first section of the track, standing in the center on a snowy ramp. Like the Child of Light during the slide from the Valley Rest, it is auto-collected by sliding through or near it.

=== The Citadel ({{#var:the_citadel}})<span id="Citadel"><span id="The Citadel"></span></span>===
The Citadel can be reached through the Spirit Gate to the left of the Frozen Lake that is connected to the platform by a bridge. The player is required to have found two Ancestor Spirits in the Valley of Triumph to enter.

<gallery widths="125" spacing="small" captionalign="center" orientation="landscape" hideaddbutton="true" mode="nolines">

*'''Valley WL 5''': Fly to the highest tower on the left, behind the golden rings. The Child of Light is standing in the leftmost tower.
*'''Valley WL 6''': From Valley WL 5's location, cross the bridge to the other high tower on the right. Jump out of the window facing away from the golden rings and descend. on the wall of the tower there are four alcoves, and the Child of Light is standing in the lowest one. This Child of light is auto-collected upon contact.

To get to the Upper Valley Track, leave the Citadel through the building at the back of the area.
