*'''Forest WL 3''': Go to the first gazebo ahead. The tree to the right of it has glowing [[Flora,_Minerals_and_Elements_of_Sky#Light_Bloom | Light Blooms]] growing on it. Use them as a ladder to reach a treehouse platform with a Child of Light in it.
*'''Forest WL 4''': From the Forest WL 3 location, look to the left; the Child of Light will be visibly standing on a platform on a tree. fly towards it or use the Light Blooms on the tree nearby to reach it.
*'''Forest WL 5''': From the first gazebo, to the left, go to the next gazebo behind the hill of [[The Treehouse]] door, where [[Blushing Prospector]] can be found. There's a cave nearby on the wall in the hillside blocked by a [[Darkness Plant]] at front, pointed by the ramp (Sightly longer than other with no path on it). Clear the Darkness to find the Child of Light.
*'''Forest WL 6''': Follow the course of the river to the end of Forest Brook, The Child of Light standing right before the exit to the next area.

===Boneyard ({{#var:boneyard}})<span id="Tree Tunnels"><span id="Boneyard"><span id="Broken Bridges"></span></span></span>===
To reach the Boneyard area, cross the Forest Brook and follow the course of the river to a split in the rocks. This area has a large tunnel/pipe at the beginning and a large lake with a broken bridge at its end.

*'''Forest WL 7''': Fly on top of the first big pipe at the beginning of the area. From the middle of the pipe on the right a hollow tree can be found with the Child of Light inside it.
*'''Forest WL 8''': Go to the stump on the left, other way of the first big pipe. A tall tower with two floors can be seen far away. At least 4 Ancestor Spirits from the Forest need to be found to enter, but this Child of Light can also be collected from outside the Spirit Gate.
*'''Forest WL 9''': Continue the main course. At the stilted temples with golden spires standing in the water, light the buttons in each of these to raise the bridge or fly to the last one that will have The Child of Light in it.

===Elevated Clearing ({{#var:elevated_clearing}})<span id="Elevated Clearing"></span>===
To reach the Elevated Clearing, go to the right of the first gazebo in the Forest Brook area or through a gate under the roots of a large tree on the right to the large lake in the Boneyard. Both entrances require the player to have found all eight Ancestor Spirits in the Hidden Forest.

*'''Forest WL 11''': The hollow tree on the right of the lake has a platform around it. Fly up, cross the rope bridge and find a Child of Light at the end of the platform.

===Forest Cavern ({{#var:forest_cavern}})<span id="Underground Cavern"><span id="Forest Cavern"></span></span>===
An area of the Hidden Forest accessible from the Elevated Clearing or the Wind Paths. The Elevated Clearing entrance requires two players to enter, but all the Children of Light in this area can be collected without assistance from other players.

*'''Forest WL 13''': From the entrance, this is the second cave to the left, after the cave where Forest WL 14 is found. The first Child of Light is located high up in this cave above plenty butterfly jars. As with the entrance to the Forest Cavern, this Child of Light can be accessed after burning the lid on the large butterfly jar hidden behind a [[Darkness Plant]] at the back of the cave, then release a [[Deep Call]] to receive a lift from the released Butterflies; the Child of Light is not reachable otherwise.
*'''Forest WL 14''': This Child of Light is located in a gated area to the bottom right of the entrance.
*'''Forest WL 15''': To the left of the entrance, the exit to the Wind Paths is located - recognized by the birds streaming from this area down into the Forest Cavern. At the very top there is a gated structure, which so far leads nowhere. There is a Child of Light standing on a pillar in front of it. For new players and those low on flight energy it can be quite hard to reach, though it may help to start from a glowing [[Flora,_Minerals_and_Elements_of_Sky#Light_Bloom | Light Bloom]] across from the opening. Additionally, the birds streaming down will recharge the cape's energy.
*'''Forest WL 16''': The Child of Light stands by the Forest Cavern exit, which is accessible at the top of the large hollow structure. Utilize the streams of Birds to recharge the cape's energy in order to fly up the structure.
