*'''Wasteland WL 1''': The Child of Light is standing on a strip of land reaching into the lake in front of the fortress.
*'''Wasteland WL 2''': Above the point where you entered the Realm there are some broken columns connected by a stone slab. The Child of Light is standing on top of it. It is hard to reach when playing alone, though the surrounding terrain can be used to gain a higher starting point. For instance, the mound of sand to the direct left of the landing area can be used to access the stone monument to the left of the Child of Light. From there, the player needs approximately 1-2 flaps to reach the next monument (with the Child of Light). Use the sparkle left behind by Wasteland WL 1 to recharge cape energy if needed. Another relatively simple way of reaching this WL if you have at least 8 wings, with no outside assistance. Starting from the top of the ruined building by the gates to Graveyard - fly horizontally to the middle of the arch with the WL on it, maintaining height with conservative flaps.

===The Graveyard ({{#var:the_graveyard}})<span id"The Graveyard"><span id="Upper Swamp"><span id="First Krill Area"></span></span></span>===
Enter the Fortress. Be cautious when proceeding in these areas. There is one Dark Dragon in the Graveyard Entrance area and four in the Graveyard that can attack the player and make them lose Winged Light (see [[Creatures]] page for details). To get to the Graveyard, slide down the hill. Going into the pipe to the right will lead to the Crab Fields area.

*'''Wasteland WL 3''': Inside, one Child of Light is standing on a little island in the lake to the right of the main building.
*'''Wasteland WL 4''': After crossing the lake area, there is a Child of Light standing in an alcove to the right of the exit.
*'''Wasteland WL 5''': In the Graveyard, one Child of Light is on top of a hill to the right of the big bones. It may be helpful to first fly up the side of the pipe leading into the Crab Fields and then jump down on the other side to reach the Child of Light.
*'''Wasteland WL 6''': Enter the main hollow structure in the middle of the lake, the Child of Light is on the far side of it, high up on a ledge, best seen from the back of structure. One of the Dark Dragons will patrol this ledge and thus players should be cautious while recharging in the sparkle left behind.
*'''Wasteland WL 7''': Another Child of Light is in a smaller pipe-like structure to the left of the main central structure.
*'''Wasteland WL 8''': There is a Child of Light near the stairs leading into the exit (where white birds are flying) on the other side of the lake. A Dark Dragon will also patrol this spot.

===Crab Fields ({{#var:crab_fields}})<span id="Crab Fields"></span>===
There are three ways to access the Crab Fields area via the Graveyard, for two of them Spirits from the Golden Wasteland will be needed to be found in order to pass through. 

*First way: through a pipe on the right of the slope that leads to the Graveyard. This pipe is blocked with a Spirit Gate that requires one Ancestor Spirit from Wasteland to open.
*Second way: Above the previously mentioned pipe with the Spirit Gate, there is another pipe that isn't blocked in any way. However, this pipe may be slightly difficult to reach with low wing energy.
*Third way: In the Graveyard area proper, go right along the rocks past Wasteland WL 9 and look for an elevated platform made out of bricks, there should be a round pipe with a Spirit Gate on this platform. The gate needs one Ancestor Spirit from Wasteland.

*'''Wasteland WL 9''': Find the place where there is a tear in the hull of the ship that is spilling lots of golden jars, the Child of Light is standing on top of them, inside the ship.
*'''Wasteland WL 10''': Fly to the very far end of the area along the rock wall leading into the Graveyard area, towards a tower. The Child of Light is standing opposite the tower high up in a cave in the cliff face. This Child of Light is not easy to reach without a friend (or a high cape level), but it might help to climb the tower and fly from there. Some players also choose to enter from the Graveyard area's second entrance (without the Spirit Gate), as it provides higher starting ground.
*'''Wasteland WL 11''': Go along the rock wall beyond the shipwreck in the direction of the Wasteland Battlefield. The Child of Light is standing beyond the utmost corner of the wall, by the sea and in the vicinity of some pipes. It may be difficult to spot far away, as it is slightly obscured by rocks and pipes.

===The Battlefield ({{#var:the_battlefield}})<span id="The Battlefield"><span id="Wasteland Battlefield"></span></span>===
The entrance to the Wasteland Battlefield is located at the very end of the Graveyard, between two large towers and a staircase that leads to a windy tunnel, where Wasteland WL 8 is located. When entering from the Crab Fields, go towards the big bundle of pipes behind the wreck and climb over the top to reach the Battlefield.

The Battlefield does not have any Dark Dragons patrolling initially, but after activating the gate of the Temple (or get close enough when it has already been lit by someone else), three Dark Dragons will be released and roam the area. Thus it is recommended not to go anywhere near the gate before finishing to explore the area.

*'''Wasteland WL 12''': There are two main buildings: the far away ending Temple that is still intact and a ruined fortress/temple surrounded by a moat. Climb the walls of the ruined fortress and the Child of Light is standing to the left (looking from the inside of the building) of the main tower. The easiest way up is to climb the right hand wall that forms steps.
*'''Wasteland WL 13''': In front of the main Temple there are several towers. The Child of Light is behind the tower to the right of the main gate.
